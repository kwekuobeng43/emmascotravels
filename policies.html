Emmasco Travels Policies

Refund Policy

  1. Full refunds will be issued for cancellations made at least 30 days prior to departure.
  2. Partial refunds (50%) will be issued for cancellations made between 15-29 days prior to departure.
  3. No refunds will be issued for cancellations made within 14 days of departure.

Cancellation Policy

  1. Cancellations made 30 days or more prior to departure will incur no penalty.
  2. Cancellations made 15-29 days prior to departure will incur a 20% cancellation fee.
  3. Cancellations made within 14 days of departure will incur a 50% cancellation fee.
